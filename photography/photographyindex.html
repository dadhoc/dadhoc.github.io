"Sprites"
02 Photos

Back in 2016, I had the idea to edit a bunch of photos from a trail near where I lived at the time by adding classic game sprites to the photos.
Back then I called the folder "Sprites" (as in, the little fairy things. Get it? I still think it's kind of a cute idea hehe.) so that's what I'll call the album here.
I only ever made two of these edits, archived here. I'll update the album if I ever choose to revisit the project.
